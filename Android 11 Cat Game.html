App Description: Android 11 Cat Game allows you to collect a large herd of cats!  Each cat is unique and wants your affection...and your food!  These cats are low-maintenance and only require food and water, though they don't mind some quality catnip on the side - setting out cat toys will attract more cats.  Pick your favorite cat, name them, and share pictures of them with your friends!
